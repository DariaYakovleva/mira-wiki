## Target Users

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Teams on Telegram" icon="users">
    Teams that live in Telegram and want AI embedded directly in their workflow — without switching to another tool.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Power Users" icon="bolt">
    Heavy users of Claude or GPT who want an agent that actually *executes*, not just responds. Mira takes action.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Founders & Operators" icon="rocket">
    Founders and operators who want AI automation without server setup, DevOps, or engineering overhead.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SMBs" icon="building">
    Small and medium businesses that need a procurement and ops layer but can't afford a dedicated team.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Use Cases by Role

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="rocket" title="Founder / Startup">
    - Automate investor updates and follow-ups
    - Manage inbound leads via Telegram Business
    - Run weekly standups and generate structured notes
    - Monitor competitors and surface key news daily
    - Execute on-chain transactions without leaving Telegram
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="users" title="Team / Operator">
    - Summarize group chat discussions and extract action items
    - Schedule meetings across time zones via calendar integration
    - Pull data from Notion, Sheets, or GitHub on demand
    - Automate recurring reports and status updates
    - Delegate inbound messages to Mira during off-hours
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="bolt" title="Power User / AI Enthusiast">
    - Switch between GPT, Claude, and Qwen models per task
    - Enable Private Mode for sensitive conversations
    - Chain agents for complex multi-step workflows
    - Generate images, videos, and music without leaving Telegram
    - Build personal automations with Skills
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="building" title="SMB / Business">
    - Connect Telegram Business for automated customer communication
    - Qualify leads and book meetings automatically
    - Manage crypto payments and on-chain transactions
    - Reduce coordination overhead with AI-powered scheduling
    - Get structured summaries of customer conversations
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
