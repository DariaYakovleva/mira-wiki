## Personal Assistant

Mira builds **structured memory**, understands your preferences, and adapts over time. The more you use it, the more it feels like your own assistant — not a generic chatbot.

- Remembers your name, role, goals, and preferences
- Adapts tone and format to how you like to communicate
- Proactively surfaces relevant information
- Runs recurring tasks on your behalf

## Available in Any Chat

Users simply type **`@mira`** in any Telegram message field to activate the agent instantly — in personal chats, group chats, or channels.

No need to open a separate app or switch context.

## Business Management

Professionals can connect their **Telegram Business account** to Mira and delegate:

- **Inbound/outbound conversations** — Mira handles messages on your behalf
- **Lead qualification** — filters and scores incoming inquiries
- **Meeting booking** — schedules via calendar integrations
- **Follow-ups** — sends timely follow-up messages automatically
- **Team coordination** — structured notes, action items, status updates

## Fun in Group Chats

Mira understands **multi-user dynamics**:

- Retains shared context across the group
- Summarizes long discussions on demand
- Participates like a digital teammate
- Knows who said what and when

```
@mira summarize the last 2 hours of this chat
@mira what are the open action items?
@mira draft a reply to what Alex just said
```

## Agent-to-Agent

Users can connect **specialized agents** to Mira for professional tasks:

- Chain with external LLMs for complex workflows
- Connect domain-specific agents (legal, finance, code review)
- Multi-step automations with structured outputs
