## What is Mira?

Mira (`@mira`) is the first consumer AI agent inside Telegram that turns messaging into an **execution layer** — connecting memory, content creation, crypto tools, and real-world actions across apps and services.

Built on top of Telegram's social graph, Mira enables users to think, create, and act — across personal and group chats seamlessly and with secure data handling.
