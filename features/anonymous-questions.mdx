# Anonymous Questions

Mira lets you send and receive anonymous questions through Telegram — no extra apps, no accounts, no exposure.

## How it works

1. You get a personal anonymous question link
2. Share it anywhere — Telegram bio, Instagram, Twitter
3. Anyone with the link can ask you a question anonymously
4. Mira delivers the question to your chat with an AI-generated pre-answer
5. You decide: answer, clarify, decline, or pay to reveal who asked

## Getting your link

```
Give me my anonymous question link
```

Mira generates a unique `t.me/mira?start=ask_...` link for you.

## Asking anonymously

To ask someone an anonymous question:

```
Ask @username: What's your favorite thing about London?
```

The recipient gets the question with an AI-generated pre-answer based on what Mira knows about them.

## Answering

When you receive an anonymous question, you can:

- **Answer** — reply publicly or privately
- **Clarify** — ask a follow-up question to the anonymous sender
- **Decline** — politely decline to answer
- **Reveal** — pay tokens to discover who asked

## Privacy

<Info>
  Your identity is never revealed to the question recipient unless you choose to reveal it. Anonymous questions are processed without storing personal data.
</Info>
