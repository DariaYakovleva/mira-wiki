## Voice messages

Send Mira a voice message — she'll transcribe it and reply in text (or voice, if you ask).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Record a voice message">
    Use Telegram's built-in voice recorder to send a message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mira transcribes it">
    Mira automatically converts your speech to text.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get a reply">
    Mira responds in text. Ask *"reply with voice"* to get an audio response.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Text to speech

Ask Mira to read any text aloud:

```
Read this to me as a voice message: [your text]
```

### Available voices

| Voice | Style |
|-------|-------|
| `nova` | Energetic and bright (default) |
| `alloy` | Versatile and neutral |
| `onyx` | Deep and resonant |
| `shimmer` | Clear and melodic |
| `sage` | Authoritative and sharp |
| `coral` | Clear and bright |

## Generate music & songs

Mira can compose original music from a text description — **free of charge**.

```
Create a jazz song about coffee and Monday mornings
Make an upbeat electronic track, 30 seconds
Write and sing a song about [topic]
```

<Tip>Include lyrics in your request and Mira will sing them!</Tip>

## Video transcription

Send a video — Mira will transcribe the audio track. Useful for:
- Summarizing video content
- Translating spoken content
- Getting subtitles
