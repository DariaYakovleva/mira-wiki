## Overview

Mira is powered by state-of-the-art language models (GPT by OpenAI in normal mode, Qwen3-32B in private mode). She can answer questions, explain complex topics, write text, analyze data, and hold multi-turn conversations with full context.

## Conversation memory

Mira remembers important things about you across conversations:

- Your **name, location, occupation**
- Your **preferences and interests**
- **Facts** you share (e.g. "I prefer short answers", "I'm learning Spanish")

<Note>
  Memory is private by default. In group chats, facts are visible to other members.
</Note>

## Private Mode

For sensitive conversations, Mira offers **Private Mode** — powered by Qwen3-32B running on the [Cocoon network](https://cocoon.network), a decentralized confidential compute infrastructure.

- All requests processed in **TEE nodes** (Trusted Execution Environment)
- Data is **not stored** on external servers
- Enable in the Mira mini-app → *Discover* button
