## Step 1: Open Mira in Telegram

Mira lives in Telegram — no installation required.

<Card title="Open Mira" icon="paper-plane" href="https://t.me/mira" horizontal>
  Click to open Mira in Telegram
</Card>

## Step 2: Say hello

Just type anything — a question, a task, or a creative idea. Mira will respond instantly.

```
Hey Mira, what can you do?
```

<Tip>You can also send a voice message — Mira will transcribe and reply.</Tip>

## Step 3: Connect your apps

Unlock the full power of Mira by connecting your favorite tools.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="google" title="Connect Google Workspace">
    Ask Mira: *"Connect my Gmail"* or *"Connect Google Calendar"*.
    Mira will send you an auth link — click it and you're done.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="github" title="Connect GitHub">
    Ask Mira: *"Connect GitHub"*.
    Authorize via OAuth and Mira can read repos, create issues, push code.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="book" title="Connect Notion">
    Ask Mira: *"Connect Notion"*.
    Mira can read and write your Notion pages and databases.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
