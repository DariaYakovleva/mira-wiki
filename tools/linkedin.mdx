# LinkedIn

Connect your LinkedIn account and manage your professional presence through Mira.

## Connect

```
Connect my LinkedIn
```

## Use cases

### Post updates

```
Post on LinkedIn: "Excited to announce our new AI feature — Mira now connects to 200+ apps!"
```

### Search

```
Search LinkedIn for product managers in London
Find people who work at Google in engineering
```

<Tip>LinkedIn posts work best when you give Mira a draft or key points — she'll polish the text for professional tone.</Tip>
