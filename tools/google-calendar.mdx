# Google Calendar

Connect your Google Calendar and manage your schedule through Mira.

## Connect

```
Connect my Google Calendar
```

## Use cases

### Check schedule

```
What's on my calendar today?
Show me my meetings for this week
Am I free tomorrow at 2pm?
```

### Create events

```
Create a meeting with Sarah for Friday at 2pm
Schedule a 30-minute call with the design team on Monday at 10am
Block 3-5pm tomorrow for deep work
```

### Find free time

```
Find a free slot next week for a 1-hour call
When am I free this Friday?
```

### Manage events

```
Move my 3pm meeting to 4pm
Cancel the meeting with John tomorrow
Add a reminder: bring documents to the Friday meeting
```

<Tip>Mira knows your timezone, so *"tomorrow at 9am"* just works — no need to specify UTC.</Tip>
