## Time-saving workflows

### Content creation

```
Write a LinkedIn post about our product launch, casual tone, 150 words
Create 5 tweet variations about AI in education
Generate a blog outline: "10 Tips for Better Remote Work"
```

### Visual content

```
Generate a 9:16 image for an Instagram story about summer sale
Edit this photo — make it look like a watercolor painting
Create 4 carousel slides about productivity tips
```

### Video & audio

```
Generate a 15-second video of clouds moving over a city skyline
Create a lo-fi beat for my YouTube background
Download this Instagram Reel and summarize it
```

### Marketing

```
Post on LinkedIn: "Excited to share our Q4 results!"
Create an ad copy for A/B testing our new feature
Write a newsletter intro about this month's highlights
```

## Popular use cases

- **Carousel creation** — generate multiple slides for Instagram/LinkedIn
- **Brand voice** — create a skill that writes in your exact tone
- **Repurposing** — turn a blog post into tweets, LinkedIn posts, and newsletter content
- **Hashtag research** — find trending hashtags for your niche
- **A/B testing copy** — generate multiple variations of ad text
- **Thumbnail design** — generate eye-catching thumbnails
- **Podcast notes** — summarize audio into show notes
- **SEO optimization** — rewrite content with target keywords

<Tip>Create a skill called "Content Engine" that generates posts in your brand voice for any platform.</Tip>
